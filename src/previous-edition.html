First Edition - A New Beginning

We are proud to present the first edition of the International Conference on Application of Information Technologies in Engineering, Management, and Science (ICAI-TEMS). This conference represents the beginning of an exciting journey to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange among researchers, practitioners, and professionals across diverse fields.

The inaugural edition is being hosted by the University of Calabria, a leading academic institution located in the picturesque city of Rende, Southern Italy. Renowned for its rich cultural heritage and dynamic academic environment, Rende provides the perfect backdrop for this groundbreaking event, bringing together innovative minds from around the world.
